# HMP Logo Usage Rules

## Critical Rule

Do not create, redraw, reinterpret, or substitute an AI-generated HMP logo.

Use Andrew-created official HMP logo assets only when creating final branded materials.

## Placeholder Rule

If the official logo file is unavailable, use:

```text
logo/placeholder/hmp_logo_placeholder_web_master.svg
```

The placeholder must remain clearly labeled as a placeholder.

## Do Not

- Do not invent new HMP lettering.
- Do not create AI-generated replacement logos.
- Do not reinterpret the logo shape.
- Do not use the placeholder as a final logo.
- Do not place the logo on busy mural imagery without a clean background panel.

## Final Materials

Before final export, replace placeholder logo files with the approved official HMP logo files.
